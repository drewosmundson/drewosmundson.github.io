About Me

I'm a passionate full-stack developer with a love for creating innovative web solutions that make a real difference. With years of experience in modern web technologies, I specialize in building scalable, user-friendly applications that combine beautiful design with robust functionality.

My journey in software development started in my 8th grade STEM class. We were tasked to make a simple game using Scratch. I fell in love with the challenge and the rush of finally getting a loop block to work. Programing my TI-84 in High School> I found math so tedious I created multiple programs for finding the area of a circle, angles, to help me solve geomenty questions was the next step and from then i knew that programming was what I wanted to do with my life.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or enjoying the outdoors. I'm always excited to take on new challenges and collaborate with others who share my passion for building amazing things.
